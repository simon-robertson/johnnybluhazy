Johnny Bluhazy

Johnny Bluhazy was born in 1992, in England. Due to a turbulent childhood, he has lived in many counties south of the country and has spent much time travelling in foreign lands as well. Living somewhat of an unconventional life, experiencing many different things, Johnny has found his passion in creative writing and wishes to go on expressing himself in this way.

Bluhazy captures, at times poetically, an uneasy life that begins from childhood; where a young boy is witnessing some unpleasant scenarios at home and facing changes, alongside his sister. As we all know, life continues on, time passes, and things start to look up. The boy spends quality time with his mother who he adores and, although things aren't perfect, the family manages.

Reaching adolescence, you can see him navigate through life, but then he faces a traumatic turn and, on from then, you see how he attempts to understand life and the ways in which he can take part in it. Adulthood sees him continue to search for love in a world where he sees many problems. Strength and perseverance aren't something in life that stops after you finish a chapter.

This story has obstacles in the form of heartbreak, unfavourable family relations and situations, travel induced serenity between hard bouts of being alive and shows there is both cruelness and kindness in life.
